What diseases are most prevalent in a given area?

Hover over the states to find the data






% Diseases in states

Data Sets

  • H-DS-1: All India (from 2000 to 2011) and State-wise (2010 and 2011) number of cases and deaths due to specified diseases (Acute Diarrhoeal Diseases, Malaria, Acute Respiratory Infection, Japanese Encephalitis, Viral Hepatitis)

Methodology

  • For each state in the year 2011, average number of cases across all diseases is computed.
  • The disease with number of cases more than the average count is declared as prevalent.
